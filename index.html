What is FoA?

Festival Of Agroindustry 2026

Festival Of Agroindustry is the largest arcade gaming competition featuring various classic game categories. We provide an unforgettable gaming experience with an authentic retro atmosphere.

Each participant has the opportunity to showcase their skills, compete with the best players, and win prestigious awards.

Competition Categories

NSAC

A national competition within FOA targeted at high school students across Indonesia. Structured across four progressive stages from preliminary to grand final, NSAC challenges participants in conceptual understanding, analytical thinking, and practical skills in agroindustry.

NAC

A national competition within FOA for university students to present critical and creative solutions in agroindustry. It consists of three sub-competitions: Business Case Competition, Essay Competition, and Infographic Competition.

IAC

An international competition within FOA for university students, featuring two categories: Essay Competition, which requires abstract and full paper submission before presentation, and Idea Pitch Competition, where selected participants pitch their innovation directly at the final stage with a digital or physical prototype.
