What is FoA?

Festival Of Agroindustry 2026

Festival Of Agroindustry is the largest arcade gaming competition featuring various classic game categories. We provide an unforgettable gaming experience with an authentic retro atmosphere.

Each participant has the opportunity to showcase their skills, compete with the best players, and win prestigious awards.

Join thousands of gamers from all over the archipelago in the biggest arcade gaming celebration of the year!

Competition Categories

NSAC

National SAC Championship - A national-level competition for regional champions with the highest playing standards.

NAC

National Arcade Cup - An open arcade tournament for all skill levels with exciting prizes awaiting the winners.

IAC

International AISAC - An international competition bringing together the best gamers from various countries in Asia.
